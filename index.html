Get to know about our event

South African Music Week began its journey in Canada with a quest to nurture a cultural exchange between South Africa and Canada. That process to establish South African Music Week (SAMW) began in 2018 when Michael Moeti was invited by Canadian Music Week (CMW) CEO, Neill Dixon, to act as its business coordinator in South Africa.
